Our Story

Built on Trust. Backed by Gold.

GoldNest was founded with a single mission — to make gold investment accessible, transparent, and secure for every Indian, regardless of their budget.

Our Mission

Democratizing Gold Investment

For centuries, gold has been India's most trusted form of wealth. Yet, for millions of Indians, gold investment remained out of reach — limited by high minimum purchases, storage concerns, and lack of access to fair pricing.

GoldNest was born to change that. We believe every Indian deserves access to gold — whether they want to invest ₹100 or ₹10 lakh. Our platform combines the reliability of physical gold with the convenience of digital technology.

Founded in 2024 in Vadodara, Gujarat, we've grown to serve 500+ investors and partner with 300+ jewelry shops across India — and we're just getting started.
