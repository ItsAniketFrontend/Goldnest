Common Questions

How long does account setup take?
+

The entire process — download, registration, and KYC — takes under 5 minutes. Digital KYC verification is instant for most users. In rare cases of manual review, it may take up to 24 hours.

Is my KYC data safe?
+

Absolutely. Your KYC data is encrypted with 256-bit SSL and stored on secure servers. We never share your personal data with third parties without your explicit consent. We comply fully with India's data protection laws.

You can start buying digital gold for as little as ₹100 — which gives you approximately 0.01 grams of 24K gold at current prices. For Gold SIP, the minimum is ₹500 per month.

You can sell your digital gold instantly at live market rates via the app. The money is credited to your registered bank account within 1-2 business days.
