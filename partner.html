Requirements

Who Can Be a Partner?

We welcome jewelry shops, gold retailers, and related businesses that meet our quality standards. Our goal is a trusted, certified partner network.

  • ✓
    BIS Registration
    Must hold a valid Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) hallmarking registration.
  • ✓
    GST Registration
    Valid GST registration is mandatory for all partners.
  • ✓
    Physical Retail Presence
    A verified physical shop location in India (home-based setups not accepted).
  • ✓
    Gold or Jewelry Business
    Primary business must be in gold, silver, platinum, or related precious metals.
  • ✓
    Smartphone / Internet Access
    Required to manage the partner dashboard and receive redemption requests.
